Why This Exists

Meet Sammi ♡

This did not start as a website. It started with heartbreak, love, grief, and the need to turn pain into something that could help someone else.

I know what it is like to love someone through addiction. I know the confusion, the anger, the fear, and the silent prayers no one else hears.

After writing my first book, I realized my story was not the only one that needed to be told. Every day, there are families, friends, people in recovery, and people grieving carrying stories they have never spoken out loud.

Your Story, Their Story, Our Story was created to give those stories a place to live.

With love and hope,
Sammi Hurst ♡

Author | Advocate | Founder of Your Story, Their Story, Our Story
