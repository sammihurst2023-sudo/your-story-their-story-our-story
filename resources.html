Resources

You do not have to go through this alone. Help exists. Support exists. Hope still exists.

If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, call or text 988 now.

This page is here for anyone touched by addiction, recovery, grief, or the weight of loving someone through it. These resources can help you find crisis support, treatment options, family support, recovery meetings, and grief support.

988 Lifeline

Free, confidential crisis support available 24/7 for anyone experiencing emotional distress or suicidal thoughts.

Visit 988 Lifeline

Confidential treatment referral and information for mental health and substance use disorders.
